The Process

How it works — start to payout

Six steps from first contact to ISK in your wallet. The program is intentionally designed to put as little on you as possible once you're set up.

1
Reach Out & Apply
Get in touch with Hamektok Hakaari — Discord is the quickest route, but in-game chat works just as well. Let us know what you produce, your rough monthly output, and what you're looking for. No obligation required to start the conversation.
2
Terms Agreed Together
We agree on your item, slot type, rate structure, and expected volume. During the pilot, each partner starts with one item slot to ensure every member gets a fair chance to participate. Additional slots open after the pilot phase.
3
Send Your Item Exchange Contract
Create an in-game Item Exchange Contract and address it to Rax Hakaari. Contracts must go to this character specifically — it keeps a clean paper trail in our system and gives you a clear record on your end as well.
⚠ Always contract to Rax Hakaari
4
Contract Accepted & Logged
Once the contract is accepted, your delivery is recorded against your account in our system. This establishes the foundation of your payout record — every unit you send in is tracked from the moment it arrives.
5
Priced & Listed for Sale
Your goods are listed via Item Exchange Contract at a price informed by current regional market conditions. We keep tabs on supply, demand, and competitive pricing to make sure your goods are positioned to move — not sit.
6
ISK Paid to Your Wallet
Your share of each sale is transferred directly to your character in-game. During the pilot, payments go out as sales come in. As volume grows, a regular payout cadence will be established and communicated to all partners.
Accepted Goods

What we do and don't accept

The program is launching with Planetary Industry commodities. Additional categories will be introduced as the program grows. For now, here is what qualifies.

✓   Accepted at Launch

Planetary Industry — Processed Tiers

All processed and refined PI tiers are welcome. Whether you're running basic refinery chains or operating advanced factory planets, there is a slot for you. Goods must be processed to at least P1 before they're eligible.

P1 — Refined P2 — Processed P3 — Specialized P4 — Advanced

Additional commodity categories — coming as the program expands

✕   Not Accepted

Raw Planetary Extracts

P0 raw extracts are not accepted. They are far too bulky to handle efficiently and there is no current demand for them through this channel. Run your extracts through at least one processing step — even basic P1 refining — before bringing them into the program.

Slot Types

Choose the arrangement that fits your output

Two slot types are available. The right choice depends on how consistently you produce and whether you want to be the sole supplier of your item or share that space with other members. Each slot type pairs naturally with a different pricing model — more on that below.

★ Exclusive

Exclusive Slot

You are the sole supplier of your chosen item through the program. No other partner consigns the same commodity while your slot is active, giving your goods priority positioning and buyers a single reliable source.

  • Your item is reserved — no competing partner stock
  • Best suited to consistent, committed producers
  • Stronger positioning and more predictable sales flow
  • Expected volume is agreed upfront and forms the basis of your rate
Default Pricing: Flat Rate
◆ Shared

Shared Slot

Multiple members can supply the same item. Each partner's contribution is tracked individually and attributed accurately. A natural fit for part-time or variable producers who still want access to a managed sales channel without a heavy volume commitment.

  • Multiple partners can supply the same commodity
  • Sales tracked and attributed per partner individually
  • Minimum batch sizes agreed per item during onboarding — keeps the process clean for everyone
  • Volume estimates discussed upfront, not enforced as a hard floor
Default Pricing: Commission Per Sale
Pricing Models

How you get charged for the service

Two pricing models are available. The default recommendation pairs naturally with each slot type, but both are negotiable. Here is how they work, and why you might choose one over the other.

Flat Rate

Recommended for Exclusive Slots

A fixed periodic fee agreed at onboarding, based on your expected monthly volume, item tier, and slot type. You pay the same amount regardless of how many units sell in a given period.

This model rewards partners who stock their slot consistently. The more product you put through, the lower your effective cost per unit — which directly incentivizes keeping your slot well-stocked. It also gives both parties predictable numbers to plan around.

Works Well When
  • You produce consistently at a known volume
  • You want predictable, fixed costs
  • You're committed to keeping the slot stocked
Less Ideal When
  • Your output is irregular or uncertain
  • You're not sure yet how much you can commit to

Commission Per Sale

Recommended for Shared Slots

A percentage of each sale, deducted from the proceeds. You only pay when you earn — if nothing sells, nothing is owed. The rate is agreed individually based on the item and arrangement.

This model aligns both parties' interests directly — more sales means more for everyone. It scales naturally with output, making it fair for partners whose production varies month to month, and lower-commitment for those just getting started.

Works Well When
  • Your output varies or is hard to predict
  • You want shared risk — pay only when you earn
  • You're new to the program and want flexibility
Less Ideal When
  • You produce high volume and want a lower effective rate
  • You prefer fixed, predictable costs for planning

During the Pilot Phase

While market data is being collected and velocity is being established, all partners will operate on a flat rate regardless of slot type. This keeps things simple while we learn what moves, what doesn't, and at what pace. Once the pilot phase concludes, partners will have the option to transition to the model that best suits their arrangement — or negotiate a hybrid approach. All of this will be discussed openly as we reach that point.

Fees & Costs

Transparent costs — no surprises

Simple principle: you only pay when you are part of the program or when you earn. No hidden costs, no fine print.

Zero Entry Cost

No Joining or Setup Fee

There is no cost to apply or to get set up in the program. The conversation, the onboarding, and the slot itself do not cost you anything upfront. Costs only apply once the arrangement is active and agreed.

Agreed Individually

Flat Rate or Commission

Your service fee is either a flat periodic rate or a commission per sale — agreed individually during onboarding based on your item, slot type, and expected volume. During the pilot, all partners run on flat rate. After the pilot, the right model for your arrangement will be discussed and agreed openly.

100% Optional

Corp Donation

Partners may voluntarily contribute a portion of their earnings as a donation to the corporation. This is entirely opt-in — you decide whether to participate and you set the percentage yourself. It has no bearing on your service fee or slot terms.

Our Responsibilities

Everything we take off your plate

Once your contract lands and the arrangement is active, here is what we own from that point forward.

Receiving & Recording

  • Accepting your Item Exchange Contracts via Rax Hakaari promptly
  • Logging your delivery against your account in our system
  • Maintaining a clean record of everything received, sold, and owed
  • Providing transaction records to you on request at any time

Contract Management

  • Creating and managing Item Exchange Contracts to move your goods
  • Monitoring regional trends, pricing, and competitive conditions
  • Adjusting contract pricing as market conditions shift
  • Coordinating trade hub options with you if local demand requires it

Tracking & Transparency

  • Logging every sale against your account individually
  • Tracking exactly what you are owed at all times
  • Calculating your payout accurately against agreed terms
  • Keeping a clear record of all payments made to you

Payouts

  • ISK transferred directly to your character in-game
  • Paid out as sales come in during the pilot phase
  • Formal payout schedule established as volume grows
  • Unsold stock returned to you via contract if you exit the program
