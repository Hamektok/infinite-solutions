Infinite Solutions  Partner Program

You produce it. We sell it. You get paid — without ever touching the market.

1
Apply to the Program
Reach out to Hamektok Hakaari on Discord. Share what PI you produce, your approximate monthly volume, and what kind of arrangement you're looking for.
2
Terms Agreed Together
Commission rate, slot type, and pricing approach are set individually — no one-size-fits-all. Exclusive slots make you the sole supplier. Shared slots let multiple producers participate. Everything is transparent before anything starts.
3
Item Exchange Contract
You create an in-game Item Exchange Contract addressed to Hamektok Hakaari, listing the PI goods you're consigning. This is EVE's native secure handoff — no ISK upfront, no risk of loss.
4
Sorted into Labeled Bins
Received goods are immediately organized into dedicated labeled bins — one per partner. Items never touch a shared pile. Zero chance of mix-up between partners, and your stock is always traceable.
5
We List and Manage Everything
Your goods go live at competitive, market-informed prices. We handle repricing, relisting, and order management continuously — not just once. You do nothing after delivery.
6
ISK Paid to Your Wallet
When your goods sell, your agreed share lands directly in your character's wallet. Every transaction is logged and traceable. No guesswork, no delays — paid on demand.
What You Pay
  • No upfront fees of any kind
  • No slot reservation or storage charges
  • No listing fees — we absorb broker costs
  • Commission is negotiated individually and comes out of sale proceeds only — you never pay from your own pocket
  • Optional voluntary donation to Infinite Solutions — entirely your choice and your rate
What We Handle
  • Accepting and processing your Item Exchange Contracts
  • Sorting all received goods into your dedicated labeled bin
  • Creating and actively managing market listings
  • Monitoring prices and relisting as needed
  • Logging every sale and tracking exactly what you are owed
What You Get
  • Your agreed share of every sale, direct to your wallet
  • Zero market management time required
  • Your own dedicated bin — no item mix-up risk, ever
  • Full transaction records available on request
  • A reliable, professional sales channel for your PI output
