🏭 Infinite Solutions — PI Partner Program

Keep the alliance supplied. Earn ISK doing what you're already doing.

Recruiting PI Producers TEST Alliance Null-Sec

The problem we're solving

Every time a corp or alliance member needs PI for manufacturing, fuel, or fitting — and it isn't stocked locally — that's ISK and time walking out the door to Jita. Hauling costs add up. Availability gaps slow down operations. Local supply matters.

Infinite Solutions exists to fix that. We stock the materials our people actually need, sourced from producers inside the alliance. If you're running planets, you're already part of the solution — we just want to make it official and make it pay.

What this is

A consignment program for PI producers in corp and alliance. You produce, contract your output to us, and receive a competitive commission split on every unit sold. We handle the market side — pricing, listings, accounting — you just keep the planets running.

Why it matters

  • Local supply strengthens ops — stocked PI means faster manufacturing turnaround, fueled structures, and fewer "we're waiting on materials" moments for everyone.
  • Your output goes further here — selling locally means the ISK stays in the alliance ecosystem rather than enriching Jita traders.
  • Transparent and professional — live inventory dashboard, full ISK accounting per sale, regular payouts. You always know what sold and what you're owed.
  • No market skills needed — we do the selling. You just need to produce and contract.
  • Flexible and low-commitment — stock when you have it. No penalties for downtime. Real life happens.

How it works

  1. We have a quick conversation about what you produce and rough volumes.
  2. We agree on commission rate and a stock cap per item.
  3. You get a dedicated named container in our hangar at LX-ZOJ.
  4. Deliver via contract or direct drop — your choice.
  5. Items sell on the local market. You receive your cut each payout cycle.

Who we want

Corp and alliance members with active planetary setups who produce consistently. Ideally:

  • P1 producers with high-volume, regular output
  • P2/P3 manufacturers with a consistent monthly yield
  • P4 producers running rare or high-value items

Solo pilots, small industry groups, and alt-based operations all work. If your planets are spinning, we want to hear from you.

Open slots

We have capacity across most PI tiers right now. What's most needed shifts with alliance activity — reach out and we'll tell you exactly what has demand and what the rates look like for your specific output.

Rates

Negotiated individually based on item tier, volume, and consistency of supply. We don't post a single number because a P4 producer and a bulk P1 supplier are different conversations — both valuable, different math.

We want this to be worth your time. If the numbers don't work for you, we'll say so upfront.

Get in touch

DM me here or convo Hamektok Hakaari in-game. Just tell me:

  • What PI you're producing and roughly how much per month
  • Your current setup (dedicated alt, main, small group, etc.)
  • Any questions

No application form, no hoops. Just a conversation.
